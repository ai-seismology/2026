About the workshop

Overview

This one-day workshop brings together leading academics and industry stakeholders to explore how artificial intelligence and machine learning techniques are transforming seismological research — from earthquake detection and source characterisation to subsurface imaging and hazard assessment.

This workshop aims to foster dialogue between academia and industry, accelerate knowledge exchange, and identify open challenges at the intersection of AI, seismology and underground energy storage.

Deep learning for seismic data Earthquake detection & classification Subsurface imaging Source characterisation Physics-informed neural networks Seismic hazard & risk Generative models Foundation models in geophysics Industry applications
🎓

Academic talks

Invited presentations from researchers at the forefront of AI-driven seismology, covering both methodological advances and real-world applications.

🏭

Industry perspectives

Stakeholder contributions from Shell and other industry partners sharing operational challenges and opportunities for AI integration.

💬

Panel discussion

An open forum exploring the future of AI in seismology, barriers to adoption, reproducibility, and the path to operational deployment.
